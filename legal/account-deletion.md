This page explains how you can delete your Daily History account and what data is removed. It applies to all sign-in methods (Email/Password, Google, and Apple).

---

## How to delete your account from within the app

1. Open the **Daily History** app.
2. Go to your **Profile** (top of the home screen).
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Account**.
4. In the confirmation dialog, type **DELETE** and tap **Delete permanently**.

Your account and associated data are deleted immediately and permanently. This action cannot be undone.

## How to request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing **razvanstefan.dogaru@gmail.com** from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Account Deletion". We will process your request within 30 days.

## What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

- Your account and profile (email, username, and — for Google sign-in — your stored profile avatar);
- Your gamification data (experience points, level, streaks, achievements, daily goals);
- Your quiz attempts and answers;
- Your reading history and saved events;
- Any support messages you sent through the app.

For users who signed in with Google, the profile avatar stored on our image provider (Cloudinary) is also deleted.

## Data retention

We do not retain personal data after account deletion, except where retention is strictly required to comply with legal obligations (for example, transaction records required by tax or accounting law). Any such data is kept only for the minimum period required by law and is then deleted. Routine backups are purged on a rolling basis, normally within 30 days.

Note: payments and subscriptions are processed by Apple and Google. Subscription and purchase records held by those stores are governed by their respective policies and are not controlled by us.
